About Arthings

Empowering communities through shared resources

Our Mission

Arthings connects local communities through shared resources. We believe that sharing leads to stronger communities and more sustainable living. Our platform makes it easy to rent everyday items from your neighbors, reducing waste and building connections.

Our Values

Community First

Building connections between neighbors and fostering trust within local communities.

Trust & Safety

Verified users, transparent reviews, and secure communication for peace of mind.

Sustainability

Reducing waste and consumption by maximizing the use of existing resources.
